About The Photographer

Hey There! My name is Karen. So glad you have stopped by!

I am excited to share a little bit about me and my passions and interests.

I am a jesus lover, wife, mom, daughter, handmade jewelry maker and photographer.

I have 2 kids – one boy and one girl. Perfect little family. I have a loving and hard-working husband and

we have been married for 32 years. We were high school sweethearts.

My two passions are hand stamped jewelry making over at Mother Daughter Jewel and photography.

I am not new to photography. I have always loved taking photos, capturing moments.

I recently purchased a DSLR camera and have enjoyed learning and taking tons of photographs.

I love doing maternity, birth, fresh 48, newborn, engagement, couples, families and other areas of photography.
